Join Our Community of Brands

At Aurmoire, we believe that there is a story behind every fashion product. We would like to share your stories with our club members.

Our community is your platform to communicate the meaning of your designs. You will get to showcase your products to our club members. Just like museum curators, we emphasize on the history and design of your brand and your products.

We take care of the logistics and the distribution of your designs. Giving you a cost-efficient alternative to bring your collections to the market. Better yet, you can focus on channeling all your energy to creating new collections, styles and designs.

Contact Us:
To join our community, send us a message with your story along with your name, phone number and email address to info@aurmoire.com.
