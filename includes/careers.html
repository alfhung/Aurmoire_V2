Open Roles

There is always room in the family. We are currently looking for individuals to be leaders in our creative, business and technical teams.

Creative Team

Creative Director
In this role, you will be the leader for the creative team. Your main focus will be developing the service while contributing to multiple areas of the business. You will act as a personal stylist for our clients. You will also be our our social media content creator.
Qualifications
Since our clients will depend on the creative director for our services, this role requires extensive knowledge about fashion. You must have a passion in fashion and demonstrate such passion through previous experiences and works.
Though not required, we are looking for someone with a degree in fashion (i.e. design, styling, merchandising, textiles,etc.). However we are open for candidates with experiences in fashion and can show us that fashion is your passion.
If you have worked as a personal stylist or personal shopper, we will consider your experiences. We are also considering candidates passionate about fashion media.
For more information about this role, please don't be shy to message us at info@aurmoire.com.

Business Team

Business Director
This role contributes to the development and growth of the business. Your primary responsibility is customer development. However, sporadically, you will contribute to the financial, marketing and operations aspects of the business.
Qualifications
Passion for fashion and startups is a must. Must be a teamworker and have good communication skills. You are willing to take risks and able to learn from your failures.
This role is open for candidates with any business, artistic or technical background. For candidates with liberal arts education, you are welcome to show us any of your credentials and work. A background in MBA, finance, accounting would be a plus.
For more information about this role, please don't be shy to message us at info@aurmoire.com.

Technical Team

Technical Director
This role leads the development of the technology required for the business to operate. The focus will be on developing the platform while supporting the creative and business teams.
Qualifications
Very passionate about startups and interested in fashion.
Have a technical background through either education or experience. Able to design and develop.
For more information about this role, please don't be shy to message us at info@aurmoire.com.
